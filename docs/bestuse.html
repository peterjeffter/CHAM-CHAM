Best use of CHAMCHAM!

The app is best used by the learner with the leading of the caregiver so as to ensure that he is actually speaking and
not falling back to incoherence. The caregiver is expected to patiently guide the learner, pointing out the activitie
going on and verbalising them to slowly and articulately using short simple sentences as much as possible,
while encouraging the learner to do the same until the learner easily expresses himself in level several or all of
the scenes. At that time a higher difficulty level should be chosen.
Every time the caregiver is satisfied with the learner’s attempts, he utters the prompt clearly from the list of prompts,
or the particular phrase which he himself has saved on the app. Once the app recognises this phrase, the learner gets to watch a little animation as a reward, and a random scene will be generated.
The app can be used by persons of any language, since the app does not decide the rightness or wrongness of the learner’s
utterances. That is for the caregiver to decide.
The professional caregiver only needs to download the app once, and up to three learners can be registered on his
account. Registering additional learners will require an additional subscription.
